What is Starknet?

Starknet is a leading validity rollup-based Layer 2 scaling solution that relies on zero-knowledge Scalable Transparent Arguments of Knowledge (or zk-STARKS), which allows Starknet to prioritize privacy, scalability, and security.

Originally built as an L2 for Ethereum, Starknet has its eyes set on Bitcoin as well. It aims to become Bitcoin’s execution layer, unlocking more scalability for the world’s largest blockchain and opening up new avenues for Bitcoin DeFi.

Additionally, its goal is to bring DeFi products and services to the most prominent blockchain even before OP_CAT is activated, giving Bitcoiners a taste of what user-friendly, trustless BTCFi can look like.

Access and Manage USDC Runes (USDC•STARKNET)

USDC Runes (USDC•STARKNET) are Starknet Asset Runes, a new class of assets native to the Bitcoin network that represent non-Bitcoin assets on a 1:1 ratio. 

They establish a direct and verifiable link between assets on Starknet and their representation as Runes on Bitcoin. For example, Starknet Asset Runes are bringing the popular stablecoin USDC to the Bitcoin network, allowing anyone with an Xverse wallet to securely manage and transact with USDC.

What does the Starknet token do?

The STRK token powers the Starknet ecosystem by enabling users to pay for transaction fees, participate in governance, and more. It plays an indispensable role in maintaining the decentralization and scalability of the network.

Through Xverse, users can easily acquire and use STRK to engage with DeFi protocols and dApps on Starknet. This makes it a necessary asset for anyone taking part in Bitcoin-based Layer 2 DeFi.

What are the benefits of Starknet?

Starknet is a zk-STARK rollup Layer 2 that enhances scalability, security, and privacy for blockchain applications. By processing transactions off-chain and submitting proofs to the main chain, it reduces congestion and usage fees substantially.

Additionally, Starknet aims to become Bitcoin’s execution layer, enabling superior, trustless Bitcoin DeFi capabilities even before upgrades like OP_CAT. Through Xverse, users can access these benefits within a familiar, user-friendly Bitcoin-focused environment.
